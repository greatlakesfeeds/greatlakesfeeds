Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

GreatLakes Feeds ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at greatlakesfeeds.github.io/greatlakesfeeds and interact with our services.

By using our website, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you contact us through our website. This may include:

  • Name — so we can address you personally when responding to your inquiry.
  • Email address — to respond to your messages or send information you requested.
  • WhatsApp / phone number — if you initiate contact via our WhatsApp link, your number is shared with us through the WhatsApp platform.
  • Message content — the details of your inquiry, request, or feedback.

We do not use any automated forms that collect data in the background. We do not collect payment information directly on this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any personal data you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about our products and services.
  • Send you information you specifically requested (e.g., product specifications, pricing).
  • Improve the quality of our customer communication.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data for automated profiling or decision-making.

3. Cookies and Analytics

Our website does not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. We do not currently operate a web analytics platform (such as Google Analytics) on this site.

Your browser may store cookies set by embedded third-party services (see Section 4 below). These are governed by those third parties' own privacy policies. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website embeds or links to the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • WhatsApp (Meta Platforms Inc.) — clicking our WhatsApp contact button opens the WhatsApp application. Any communication initiated through WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. WhatsApp may collect your phone number and message metadata.
  • YouTube (Google LLC) — our "Our Story" section embeds a YouTube video. When you play this video, YouTube may set cookies and collect viewing data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. The video is loaded on demand (only after you click play) to minimise data sharing on page load.
  • Google Maps (Google LLC) — our footer includes an embedded Google Maps frame showing our location. Google may collect data when you interact with this map, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc.) — our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical data (IP address, request logs) as outlined in GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not control this data collection.

5. Data Storage and Security

We do not operate a database or server that stores personal data collected through this website. Contact information shared via WhatsApp or email is retained only within those respective communication platforms and in our internal correspondence.

We take reasonable precautions to ensure your data is handled securely; however, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to exercise caution when sharing sensitive personal information online.

6. Your Rights Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

If you are located in Kenya, you have rights under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (No. 24 of 2019), which gives you the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request that we delete your personal data, where we have no lawful reason to retain it.
  • Objection — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 7 below. We will respond within a reasonable time and in compliance with applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to external sites (press coverage, award bodies, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
